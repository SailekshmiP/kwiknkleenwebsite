The high temperature of the steam actually kills harmful microorganisms. Steam cleaning has most remarkably become one of the best and popular ways to clean the vehicle in an eco-friendly manner. Whether the head lamps have become oxidized due to regular wear and tear, head lamp restoration is the perfect remedy for refinishing aged head lamp lenses. Windshield Treatment

Windshield treatment is used for protection of the windshield of cars. With the help of this treatment, hard water spots and rusting can be eliminate. A fine protective layer is formed to provide clear vision to drive. During night as well as the rainy season, this treatment enhances to drive safely. The common treatment includes a windshield repellent coating that either repels water or fog. Any kind of liquid or gaseous forms of marks are not sticked to the glass because of this protection.

Car Deodorizing

Deodorizing is an excellent way to eliminate offensive odours from the cars. Thoroughly clean the car, once the cause of the the smell has been found and eliminated. Diagnose the cause and take preventive measures to remove the bad odour from the car. Vehicle’s cabin air filter is also a common source of unpleasant car odour. By deodorizing, the air quality of the cabin also can be improved, otherwise external pollution on road can contaminate the air quality within the car. Deodorizing provides a fresh and longlasting result inside the car. A variety of odours are destroyed by eliminating the root sources of the odour. Car Deodorizing makes the air quality inside better and the entire drive becomes a comfortable experience.

A proper waxing and polishing allows refurbishing of paint fading and makes the car look shiny. Provides protection against scratches and protection from UV exposure and sunlight.
